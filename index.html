Video Conferencing

The easiest way to meet online


Security



KeepTalking was built to have a private video conferencing experience. What you say in your meeting, stays in your meeting. No one is able to access your meeting's link unless they enter a password, which you can set. For an extra layer of security, you can set a password for the meeting's link so even if an external person somehow manages to get hold of it, they will not be able to join your meeting.



Ease of use

With a minimal interface, you won't get confused with buttons and menus. You can choose the things that you need, not have unnecessary apps. To join a meeting, you can call in, or join on a computer in browser, or with the desktop application.

Take it to the big stage

Record the meeting to Dropbox or live stream it to YouTube - show your meeting to whoever you want.
